<span class="keyword">Concurrent Thought</span> is the name of [Dean Wampler's]({{site.baseurl}}/) consulting company (which is currently inactive). [Dean has been cited](https://analyticsweek.com/top-200-thought-leaders-in-bigdata-analytics/){:target="_analytics_week"} as one of the most influential people in the <span class="keyword">Big Data</span> ecosystem. More recently, he has been a technical leader at [The AI Alliance](https://aialliance.org){:target="aia"}, where he leads several technical projects and contributes to education and policy initiatives. Dean's views on all these technologies have been presented at many conferences. [His talks are available here](/papers).

The name <span class="keyword">Concurrent Thought</span> reflects the view that classic distributed programming techniques are the essential core of successful modern technologies like <span class="keyword">streaming (&ldquo;fast&rdquo;) data</span> and the engineering of successful <span class="keyword">AI/ML</span> applications.
